UX

&CHILL

NOV 13 | 21 | 29

Learn more about innovation, design-thinking, and all about user experience (UX) in this three-day workshop which will unfold over the month of November. UX&Chill aims to promote UX and its uses to various demographics, and invite experts to share about their expertise and journey in the UX industry. Make art work. Chill with UX.

an initiative of

Rhea Guntalilib

Rhea Guntalilib is a visually-impaired Digital Accessibility Specialist, working as an Accessibility Consultant at Level Access, a Usability Tester at My Blind Spot, and a Software Development Analyst at Smart Communications, Inc.. She also actively participates as a speaker in various tech-related events and as a motivational speaker when given the opportunity. Having been blind since she was 18 years old, she aims to promote digital accessibility in order to make a difference in her field especially to those who have a disability.

Jordan Deja

Jordan Deja is an Assistant Professor at De La Salle University. He is also the Co-Founder and the Chief Operations Officer of Flex Group Holdings Inc., a human-centric AI and Software Solutions. His research interests revolve around creative human-centered interaction. He is also a regular guest speaker at Google Developer Group Philippines events and other tech-related forums such as UXPH.

He specializes in design research, brand and brand identity, brand management, and marketing consultancy.

Gov Daswani

Gov Daswani is a former lecturer at the University of Asia and the Pacific. He is also the Founder and the Creative Director of an award-winning newborn apparel brand called Beginnings Baby. He is currently the Chief Innovation Officer for Vessel, a Manila-based Innovation design firm that specializes in breaking barriers across industries.

Philip Cheang

Philip Cheang is graduate of Bachelor of Fine Arts in Information Design. He is a designer, a developer, and an educator, who specializes in making games, apps, and websites, as well as teaching design. He co-founded By Implication, an independent software and game development studio which has won awards and has helped clients both locally and abroad. He has also helped create Storylark, Open Reconstruction, Wildfire, and Sakay.ph among other things. He mainly focuses on design and direction work for interfaces, graphics, and branding.
